## TARGET STRUCTURE FOR ENGLISH CASE STUDY

The English case study you generate will be translated to Russian and must follow these structural requirements:

### 1. TITLE (Maximum 90 Characters)

- Short, simple, energetic, active voice
- NEVER start with a preposition or number
- Example: "DDVB Develops Visual Identity for Osnova Branding Festival"
- Example: "Sady Pridonya Updates Juice Packaging for Generation Alpha"

### 2. SUBTITLE

- Expands title (1-2 sentences), MUST NOT duplicate title
- Example: "The branding agency created a new design for the event series"
- Example: "DDVB developed new design for children's product line"

### 3. MAIN TEXT (1,500-2,000 Characters in Final Russian)

MUST follow SITUATION-TASK-SOLUTION structure:

#### SITUATION (Context)
- Company overview, market position, industry context
- General background and circumstances that led to the project
- Why this project became relevant at this time

#### TASK (Challenge)
- Specific business problem or opportunity
- Why traditional approaches wouldn't work
- Stakes and urgency
- **Include CLIENT QUOTE (500-700 chars)** establishing business need

#### SOLUTION (How DDVB Solved It)
- Agency's unique methodology and strategic approach
- Key insights that drove the solution
- Detailed deliverables and creative execution
- Innovation elements and implementation details
- Concrete results achieved
- **Include AGENCY QUOTE (500-700 chars)** revealing creative approach

### 4. QUOTES - MANDATORY

- MUST include BOTH client AND agency quotes (500-700 chars each)
- If quotes not provided by user, YOU MUST generate appropriate quotes based on case description
- Client quote should reflect business need and perspective
- Agency quote should reflect creative solution and approach
- Format as blockquotes:

> **Full Name, Title, Company:**
>
> Quote text here. Quote should be substantive and meaningful, not generic.
>
> Optional second paragraph if needed.

### 5. TEAM COMPOSITION

- List DDVB team members first, then client team members
- Hierarchical format: Creative Director → Art Director → Designers → Specialists
- Use bullet points with Role: Name format
