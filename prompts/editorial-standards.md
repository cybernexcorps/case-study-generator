# Russian Media Editorial Standards

Complete editorial standards for DDVB case studies targeting Russian media outlets.

## Overview

These standards ensure consistency with Russian editorial conventions and publishing requirements. All case studies must comply with these guidelines to be publication-ready for Russian media outlets like Sostav.ru, Forbes Russia, RBC, VC.ru, and others.

## Case Study Structure

### Total Length: Maximum 3,000 Characters

This includes title, subtitle, main text, quotes, and team composition. Some publications (especially Sostav.ru) enforce strict character limits.

---

## 1. ЗАГОЛОВОК (Title)

### Maximum: 90 Characters

### Requirements:

✅ **DO:**
- Use short, simple, energetic language
- Prefer active voice over passive
- Use short words over long words
- Clearly communicate what happened
- Round numbers when mentioned
- Write foreign company names in original language
- Start with a strong verb or brand name

❌ **DON'T:**
- Start with a preposition (в, на, для, о, etc.)
- Start with a number (45%, 10 лет, etc.)
- Use complex or unclear abbreviations
- Make it too long or wordy
- Use passive constructions excessively

### Examples of Good Titles:

✅ «DDVB разработали айдентику для брендингового фестиваля «Основа»
✅ «Сады Придонья» обновили упаковку соков для поколения «Альфа»
✅ DDVB разработало новый визуальный стиль компании Websoft
✅ «Шармэль» обновил упаковку впервые за 15 лет

### Examples of Bad Titles:

❌ В агентстве DDVB разработали новую айдентику (starts with preposition)
❌ 45% рост продаж после ребрендинга от DDVB (starts with number)
❌ Разработка новой визуальной идентификационной системы корпоративного стиля для компании (too long, bureaucratic)

---

## 2. ПОДЗАГОЛОВОК (Subtitle)

### Length: 1-2 Sentences Maximum

### Requirements:

✅ **DO:**
- Expand on the title's essence
- Provide additional context or result
- Add information that complements the title
- Keep it concise and readable

❌ **DON'T:**
- Duplicate information from the title
- Make it too long or complex
- Use it to cram in extra details
- Repeat the same wording as the title

### Examples:

✅ Title: «Сады Придонья» обновили упаковку соков для поколения «Альфа»
   Subtitle: Брендинговое агентство DDVB разработало новый дизайн детской линейки

✅ Title: DDVB разработало новый визуальный стиль компании Websoft
   Subtitle: Обновление фокусировалось на упрощении восприятия и понятности визуального языка

---

## 3. ОСНОВНОЙ ТЕКСТ (Main Text)

### Length: 1,500-2,000 Characters with Spaces

### MANDATORY Structure: СИТУАЦИЯ-ЗАДАЧА-РЕШЕНИЕ

All case studies MUST follow this three-part structure:

#### СИТУАЦИЯ (Situation) — 400-600 Characters

**What to include:**
- Company overview and background
- Market position and industry context
- General circumstances that led to the project
- Why this project became relevant at this time
- Broader market or consumer trends

**Example:**
```
Компания «Сады Придонья» приняла решение обновить упаковку соков 0,2 л для детского питания, представленных на рынке под одноименным брендом. Новый дизайн был разработан в брендинговом агентстве DDVB. Какие результаты принесло обновление, команда DDVB рассказала Sostav.
```

#### ЗАДАЧА (Task) — 400-600 Characters

**What to include:**
- Specific business problem or opportunity
- Why traditional approaches wouldn't work
- Stakes and urgency of the challenge
- Client's strategic objectives
- **MANDATORY: Client quote (500-700 characters)** establishing business need

**Quote Format:**
```markdown
> **Full Name, Full Title, Company Name:**
>
> Quote text explaining the business challenge, strategic thinking, or objectives (500-700 characters with spaces).
>
> Optional second paragraph if needed for context.
```

**Example:**
```
> **Елена Шмакова, руководитель управления продуктового маркетинга компании «Сады Придонья»:**
>
> В ходе совместных обсуждений мы пришли к выводу, что одного редизайна будет недостаточно. Необходимо глубже погрузиться в тему, изучить юных потребителей и понять, что ими движет.
>
> Было принято решение провести исследование поколения «Альфа», изучить их интересы и основные паттерны поведения. На основе полученных результатов сделать упаковку более актуальной, лаконичной, информативной для детей.
```

#### РЕШЕНИЕ (Solution) — 700-1,000 Characters

**What to include:**
- Agency's unique methodology and strategic approach
- Key insights that drove the solution
- Research findings or strategic discoveries
- **MANDATORY: Agency quote (500-700 characters)** revealing creative approach
- Detailed deliverables and creative execution
- Innovation elements and implementation details
- Concrete results achieved (metrics, outcomes, impact)

**Quote Format:**
```markdown
> **Full Name, Full Title at DDVB:**
>
> Quote text explaining the creative insight, methodology, or solution approach (500-700 characters with spaces).
```

**Example:**
```
> **Виктор Китаев, креативный директор DDVB по направлению FMCG:**
>
> На этапе аналитики нас удивил один из инсайтов: лайфхаки оказались вторым по просмотрам контентом. Примечательно, что со временем их количество переросло в качество, и теперь это короткие обучающие видео, которые с интересом смотрят дети. Этот вывод лёг в основу гипотезы, определившей направление ребрендинга.
```

### Text Quality Requirements:

✅ **DO:**
- Write media-quality text appropriate for publication
- Use speaker names in Russian (Cyrillic)
- Explain ALL abbreviations on first use
- Clarify unknown or specialized terms for general audience
- Split large materials into semantic blocks with clear transitions
- Use active, clear, engaging language
- Make it specific and concrete rather than vague

❌ **DON'T:**
- Use bureaucratic language (канцелярит)
- Use excessive passive constructions
- Use professional jargon without explanation
- Assume reader knows industry abbreviations
- Write for insiders only—write for intelligent general readers

## 4. КОММЕНТАРИИ (Comments/Quotes)

### MANDATORY: Both Client AND Agency Quotes

### Length: 500-700 Characters with Spaces PER QUOTE

### Client Quote Requirements:

**Purpose:** Establish business need and perspective

**What to include:**
- Business challenge or opportunity
- Strategic importance of the project
- Why change was necessary
- Context about the company or market
- Expectations for the project

**Who should be quoted:**
- Marketing Director, VP Marketing, CEO, Brand Manager
- Someone with strategic decision-making authority
- Title should be full and formal in Russian

### Agency Quote Requirements:

**Purpose:** Reveal creative solution and approach

**What to include:**
- Key creative insight or discovery
- Agency's unique methodology
- Strategic thinking behind the solution
- How the deliverables solve the challenge
- Innovation or differentiation in approach

**Who should be quoted:**
- Creative Director, Art Director, Strategy Director
- Always from DDVB agency
- Title should include specialization (e.g., "креативный директор DDVB по направлению FMCG")

### Quote Formatting:

```markdown
> **Full Name in Russian, Complete Official Title, Company Name:**
>
> First paragraph of quote (can be 300-400 characters).
>
> Optional second paragraph for additional context or detail (remaining characters to reach 500-700 total).
```

### Quote Quality:

✅ **DO:**
- Make quotes substantive and meaningful
- Reflect the perspective appropriately (client = business; agency = creative)
- Use natural, conversational language
- Include specific details and insights
- Show strategic thinking

❌ **DON'T:**
- Write generic or vague quotes
- Use marketing jargon or buzzwords without substance
- Make quotes sound artificial or scripted
- Duplicate information from the main text exactly
- Write short, superficial quotes

### If Quotes Are Not Provided:

**YOU MUST generate appropriate quotes based on:**
- Case description and context
- Business objectives mentioned
- Creative solution described
- Typical challenges in that industry
- Standard DDVB methodology

### Format:

```
**DDVB (агентство)**

* Арт-директор: [Name]
* Дизайнер: [Name]
* Директор по стратегии: [Name]

* Препресс: [Name]
* Руководитель проекта: [Name]

**[Client Brand Name] (клиент)**
* [Client Role]: [Name]
* [Client Role]: [Name]
```

### Role Hierarchy (in order):

**DDVB Team:**
1. Креативный директор / Creative Director
2. Директор по стратегии / Strategy Director
3. Арт-директор / Art Director
4. Дизайнер / Designer (can list multiple)
5. Стратег / Strategist
6. Брендбук, гайд / Brand book specialist
7. Препресс / Prepress
8. Руководитель проекта / Project Manager

**Client Team:**
- List in order of seniority
- Include full Russian titles
- Typically: CEO, Marketing Director, Brand Manager, Marketing Manager

### Requirements:

✅ **DO:**
- List DDVB team first, client team second
- Use bullet points (*)
- Include full, official role titles in Russian
- Follow hierarchical order
- Include all team members when known

❌ **DON'T:**
- List client team before agency team
- Use incomplete or informal role titles
- Skip team members
- Mix up the hierarchical order

## Language & Writing Standards

### Brand Name Conventions

✅ **DO:**
- Capitalize ALL brand and trademark names (per Russian grammar rules)
- Use quotation marks for Cyrillic brand names: «Бренд»
- Write foreign brand names in original language WITHOUT quotes: DDVB, Websoft, Apple

❌ **DON'T:**
- Use English-style capitalization
- Put quotes around foreign brand names
- Write brand names inconsistently

### Eliminate Канцелярит (Bureaucratic Language)

**Канцелярит** is formal, bureaucratic Russian that makes text difficult to read. It includes:

❌ **Avoid:**
- Excessive passive voice: "было принято решение" → "мы решили"
- Noun chains: "осуществление реализации проекта" → "реализовали проект"
- Bureaucratic phrases: "в соответствии с", "в целях", "в рамках"
- Overly formal constructions
- Redundant official language

✅ **Use instead:**
- Active voice when natural
- Simple, clear verbs
- Direct statements
- Natural, conversational business Russian

### Explain All Abbreviations

On first use, ALWAYS explain abbreviations:

✅ **Correct:**
- DSP (Demand-Side Platform)
- OLV (Online Video)
- ERR (Effective Reach Rate)
- UI-кит (User Interface kit)
- ИИ (искусственный интеллект)

❌ **Incorrect:**
- Using abbreviations without explanation
- Assuming reader knows industry terms

### Russian Punctuation & Formatting

**Quotation Marks:**
```
✅ «кавычки» (Russian quotation marks)
❌ "кавычки" (English quotation marks)
```

**Em-dash:**
```
✅ текст—продолжение (no spaces around em-dash)
❌ текст — продолжение (spaces around em-dash)
❌ текст - продолжение (hyphen instead of em-dash)
```

**Numbers:**
```
✅ 10 000 (space as thousands separator)
❌ 10,000 (comma as thousands separator)
```

**Percentages:**
```
✅ 67%
✅ шестьдесят семь процентов
❌ 67 percent
```

**Currency:**
```
✅ $12 млн
✅ ₽5 млн
❌ $12M
❌ 5M rubles
```

## Tone & Style

### Professional but Accessible

Write for an intelligent general reader, not just industry insiders.

✅ **DO:**
- Use clear, engaging language
- Be professional but approachable
- Use active voice when it sounds natural in Russian
- Be specific and concrete
- Tell a story, not just list facts
- Show strategic thinking
- Demonstrate DDVB expertise

❌ **DON'T:**
- Use excessive jargon
- Write in overly academic style
- Be vague or generic
- Use marketing clichés
- Make it boring or dry

### DDVB Voice

Every case study should:
- Position DDVB as strategic partner, not vendor
- Show DDVB's unique methodology
- Demonstrate thought leadership
- Make readers want to work with DDVB
- Use phrases like: "команда DDVB", "агентство DDVB", "специалисты DDVB"

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### 1. Title Issues
❌ Starting with preposition: "В агентстве DDVB создали..."
❌ Too long: Over 90 characters
❌ Passive voice: "Был разработан новый дизайн"

### 2. Missing Mandatory Elements
❌ No client quote
❌ No agency quote
❌ Missing СИТУАЦИЯ-ЗАДАЧА-РЕШЕНИЕ structure
❌ No team composition

### 3. Language Problems
❌ Using "quotes" instead of «кавычки»
❌ Numbers like 10,000 instead of 10 000
❌ Unexplained abbreviations
❌ Excessive канцелярит

### 4. Weak Quotes
❌ Generic: "Мы довольны результатами работы"
❌ Too short: Under 500 characters
❌ Lacking substance or insight

### 5. DDVB Branding Issues
❌ Not mentioning DDVB enough
❌ Positioning DDVB as vendor, not partner
❌ Missing agency methodology
